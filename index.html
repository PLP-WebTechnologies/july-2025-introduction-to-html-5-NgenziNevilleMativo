The Legend Continues

The 2018 Subaru WRX STI is a rally-inspired sport sedan that represents the pinnacle of Subaru's performance engineering. Known for its turbocharged power, legendary all-wheel drive grip, and unmistakable aggressive styling, the STI continues Subaru's World Rally Championship heritage in street-legal form.

Subaru's performance division, STI (Subaru Tecnica International), crafted this car with pure racing DNA üèÅ. The WRX STI's combination of a 305-horsepower turbocharged BOXER engine and Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive delivers thrilling acceleration and confident handling on any road or track.

üöÄ Performance Highlights

Turbocharged BOXER Power

Under the hood, the 2018 WRX STI packs a 2.5-liter turbocharged BOXER engine producing 305 hp and 290 lb-ft of torque. This horizontally-opposed engine design provides a low center of gravity for exceptional handling dynamics and that distinctive Subaru rumble.

The twin-scroll turbocharger provides immediate throttle response, while the intercooler keeps intake temperatures cool for consistent power delivery. Power is distributed to all four wheels through Subaru's renowned Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive system and a rally-proven 6-speed manual gearbox.

2018 Model Year Enhancements

For 2018, Subaru upgraded the STI with significant performance improvements:

  • Revised Suspension: Retuned inverted struts with new spring rates for sharper handling and improved ride quality
  • Enhanced Brakes: Upgraded Brembo brakes with larger, distinctive yellow calipers for superior stopping power
  • Wheel & Tire Package: 19-inch BBS alloy wheels paired with high-performance summer tires for maximum grip
  • Steering-Responsive Headlights: LED headlights that turn with the steering wheel for better visibility through corners
  • Aerodynamic Refinements: Subtle bodywork improvements for better airflow management

Real-World Performance

The result is a car that can sprint from 0-60 mph in just 5.2 seconds and reach a top speed of 155 mph. But raw acceleration is only part of the story‚Äîthe STI's true strength lies in its ability to maintain composure through technical corners and challenging road conditions.

Whether tackling a winding mountain road, navigating through snow, or pushing hard on a racetrack, the WRX STI offers an exhilarating and confidence-inspiring driving experience that few cars can match.

üåü Key Features

Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive

Subaru's legendary AWD system is standard on every STI. Unlike part-time systems, Symmetrical AWD provides power to all four wheels continuously, ensuring maximum traction in all weather conditions and driving scenarios.

Driver-Controlled Center Differential (DCCD)

The STI features a sophisticated center differential with both automatic and manual modes. Drivers can adjust the torque split between front and rear axles using controls on the center console, optimizing performance for different driving conditions.

Multi-Mode Vehicle Dynamics Control

Choose from multiple driving modes including Intelligent, Sport, Sport Sharp, and Individual settings. Each mode adjusts engine response, transmission behavior, and stability control parameters to match your driving preferences.

Signature Styling Elements

The STI's aggressive appearance includes functional aerodynamic elements: the hood scoop channels cool air to the intercooler, the front splitter reduces lift, and the iconic rear wing provides genuine downforce at speed.
